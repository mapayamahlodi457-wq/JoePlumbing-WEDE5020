About Joe's Plumbing Services

Our History

Joe's Plumbing Services was founded in 2004 by uncle joe, a master plumber with 20 years of experience. starting with a single truck in Mbombela, the company has grown to a team of 8 certified plumbers serving residential and commercial clients across the region. Key milestones include becoming a anytime service provider in 2010 and expanding to water heater installations in 2014

Our Mission

To provide reliable, affordable, and high quality plumbing services to homeowners and businesses in Mbombela and surrounding areas. We commit to honest pricing, professional workmanship, and customer satisfaction on every job

Our Vision

To be the leading plumbing service provider in Mbombela, known for innovation, community involvement, and exellence in every service we deliver

Target Audience

Homeowners (60%): Families needing leak repairs, drain cleaning, and upgrades.
Businesses (30%): Offices/restaurants requiring commercial installs and maintenance.
Realtors (10%): Property inspections and pre-sale fixes.
